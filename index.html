    • The improtance of blood donation

    Blood is important. Red blood is the body Oxygen delivery service. And white blood cells(even thogh thry aren't really "white blood cells") help defend the body from sickness. That is why losing blood is dangerous. If you lose more than 40% of the blood in your body, you are pretty much done, because you have lost blood faster that your bones can replenish it. Blood donation can help people who have lost too much blood to survive much longer. But it is not as easy as hooking up some blodd and giving it to a patient. This is because there are different blood types. Red blood cells use these blood types like identity tags to your immune cells, that way the immune cells doesn't attack its own blood cells. Donating the right blood type and giving it to a petient is a life or death situation. There are 8 blood types in humans plus a bonus type A+, B+, AB+, O+, A-, B-, AB-, O-, and RH null aka golden blood. And while humans have 8 bloode types, dogs have 13, and cows have over 800 to deal with. Giving a patient the wrong blood type can cause and intense immune response and can cause the patient to pass away faster. But giving the right blood type could save their life. Head to the nearest dlood donation site near you and donate some blood.
